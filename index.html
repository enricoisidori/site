Designer, artist, researcher with an antidisciplinary approach, interested in vernacular forms and practices of visual unlearning.



Design practice centers on interactive installations, focusing on the conceptual aspects of projects. It also spans exhibition, editorial, motion, and web design.

Artistic practice investigates the narrative potential of objects, activated through minimal interventions, metalinguistic operations, and acts of re-signification.

Academic research delves into speculative design and visual culture, exploring non-human perspectives and descriptive models of design processes.

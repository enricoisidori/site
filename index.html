Designer, essayist, and artist with an antidisciplinary approach, interested in vernacular forms and practices of visual unlearning.

Design practice centers on interactive installations, focusing on the conceptual aspects of projects, and spans exhibition, editorial, identity, motion, and web design.

Academic research delves into speculative design and visual culture, exploring non-human perspectives and descriptive approaches to design processes.

Artistic practice focuses on the narrative potential of objects, activated through minimal interventions and subtle alterations of the everyday—acts of modification and re-signification.

Draw a line from one side
2023

Specta is the dissertation on the project created in the Final Synthesis Studio 2022/2023 at the Politecnico di Milano. The project consists of an interactive device for a fictional brand within a speculative scenario; the purpose is to use the point of view of a natural element to treat an environmental issue. We are surrounded by screens full of images on climate change that provoke eco-anxiety. To counter the spread of this feeling, Litia, a tech company, presents Specta, the first personal eco-anxiety silencer. Climate change has always been there and always will be, our biocentric vision is limited and eco-anxiety is therefore a meaningless feeling. Through Specta, it is possible to watch the same alarming events, from perpective of rocks which consider natural catastrophes as simple stratifications of debris. Climate change is not the problem, seeing it is.

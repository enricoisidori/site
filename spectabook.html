SPECTA (Editorial)

2023
We are surrounded by screens pervaded by images about climate change that provoke us ecoanxiety. Specta aims to be your personal silencer of eco-anxiety. Studies and research based on the rockâ€™s millennial knowledge, prove that climate change has always been there and always will be; that our biocentric view is limited; and that eco-anxiety is a meaningless feeling.

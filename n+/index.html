N+ Cognive Supremacy

2025
A non-invasive neural interface developed by N+ Industries to collect residual cognitive activity from the user. Using bone- conducted audio stimulation, the system captures neural micro- responses and integrates them into a distributed network. Inspired by a NATO white paper stating that “the brain is the new battlefield,” this project explores the intersection of neuroscience and conflict through an immersive and experimental artistic lens. At its core is a pioneering enterprise that envisions a future of neuromodulation technologies. Specializing in neuroweapons and NBIC advancements (Nanotechnology, Biotechnology, Information Technology, and Cognitive Science), this entity develops technologies of control. By presenting these unsettling possibilities, the project sparks critical discussions on the future of warfare, human augmentation, and the ethics of cognitive control.
